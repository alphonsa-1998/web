CAA


The Citizenship Amendment Act protests, also known as the CAA and NRC protests, the Citizenship (Amendment) Bill protests, or the CAB and NRC protests, are a series of ongoing protests in India, against the Citizenship (Amendment) Act (CAA), which was enacted into law on 12 December 2019, and against the nationwide National Register of Citizens.[36] The protests began in Assam,[37] Delhi,[38] Meghalaya,[39] Manipur, and Tripura on 4 December 2019.[25] The protests have spread across India, though the concerns of the protesters vary.[
